What is data-aos-anchor-placement?

This property decides **WHEN animation should start** based on the element position and viewport position.

👉 Top of element 👉 Center of element 👉 Bottom of element 👉 Top / Center / Bottom of screen

This gives you **EARLY or LATE animation control**.

Scroll slowly to feel the timing difference

Default – top-bottom

Animation triggers when
👉 TOP of element touches BOTTOM of viewport

✔ Most common behavior

top-bottom (Default trigger)

Early Animation – top-center

Animation triggers when
👉 TOP of element reaches CENTER of viewport

✔ Used when animation should start EARLY

top-center (Early animation)

Late Animation – center-bottom

Animation triggers when
👉 CENTER of element reaches BOTTOM of viewport

✔ Used when you want delayed animation

center-bottom (Late animation)

Balanced Animation – center-center

Animation triggers when
👉 CENTER of element meets CENTER of viewport

✔ Used for focus sections (CTA, highlights)

center-center (Balanced)

Very Late Animation – bottom-bottom

Animation triggers when
👉 BOTTOM of element touches BOTTOM of viewport

✔ Used rarely – special effects only

bottom-bottom (Very late trigger)
