Fade Animations

Fade animations are MOST USED in company projects. Smooth, clean, professional.

fade-up (from bottom)
fade-down (from top)
fade-left (from right)
fade-right (from left)

Zoom Animations

Zoom effects are good for cards, highlights, counters.

zoom-in
zoom-out

Flip (3D) Animations

Use flip animations carefully. Avoid too much in real projects.

flip-left (3D)
flip-right (3D)

Slide-Style Animations

AOS does not have pure "slide" keyword, but fade + movement behaves like slide.

slide-up (fade-up + long duration)
slide-down (fade-down + long duration)
slide-left (fade-left + long duration)
slide-right (fade-right + long duration)
