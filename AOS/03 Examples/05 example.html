Our Story

A family tradition since 1985

Bringing Italy to Your Table

Founded by the Rossi family who immigrated from Tuscany, Gusto Italiano has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over three decades. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the true flavors of Italy.

  • Fresh ingredients sourced daily
  • Handmade pasta and bread
  • Imported Italian cheeses and wines
  • Traditional wood-fired oven
Our Signature Dish

Try our award-winning "Pasta alla Nonna" - a secret family recipe that has won the "Best Pasta" award three years in a row.

Meet Our Chefs

Passionate artisans behind every dish

Marco Rossi

Head Chef & Owner

Third-generation chef from Florence, specializing in traditional Tuscan cuisine. Trained in Michelin-starred restaurants across Italy.

Pasta Artisan Tuscan Cuisine

Sofia Conti

Pastry Chef

Born and raised in Sicily, Sofia brings the sweet flavors of Southern Italy to our desserts. Her tiramisu has been featured in Food & Wine magazine.

Dessert Artist Sicilian Specialties

Pizza Master
