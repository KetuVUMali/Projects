SCRUB – SCROLL CONTROLS ANIMATION
📌 Concept 
        Scroll position = animation progress.
        Animation is directly connected to scroll position

🧠 In simple words:
      You scroll down → animation moves forward
      You scroll up → animation moves backwards
      Animation does NOT play automatically
      Scroll controls animation like a volume knob

🎯 Think like:
      “Scroll = Remote control for animation”
  
    Explain : 
    1. We create a simple GSAP animation that moves a box 300px to the right.
    2. We use ScrollTrigger to link the animation to the scroll position of the page.
    3. The scrub: true property makes the animation progress tied to the scroll position.
    4. As you scroll down, the box moves to the right, and as you scroll up, it moves back to its original position.

  
    ❌ Without Scrub
      Animation plays automatically (time-based)

    ✅ With Scrub
      Animation plays based on scroll distance

    🔑 Important Rule (Must Know)
    ⚠️ scrub works ONLY with scrollTrigger
