GSAP TimeLine :
                        TimeLine = sequence of animations
                        Multiple animations played one after another.

            Simple meaning :
                            Timeline = sequence of animations
                            Multiple animations played one after another


                    Group animation one by one complete by sequence.

            syntax :
                    gsap.timeline()

            Ex : 
                    const tl = gsap.timeline();
                    tl.to(".box1", { x: 100 })
                      .to(".box2", { y: 100 });
