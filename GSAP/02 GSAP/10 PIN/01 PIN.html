Pinned Section
    PIN – STICKY SECTION
  📌 Concept
              An Element sticks while scrolling.
              “Keep an element fixed in one position while scrolling”
  
    🧠 Explanation :
            Scroll down → section stays fixed
            Scroll up → section releases and scrolls away
            Useful for headers, menus, or important content
    
        📌 When you scroll the page:
              Content moves
              Pinned element stays at the same place
              After some scroll → it unpins and scroll continues
