GSAP Timeline Basic Example

This example demonstrates a simple GSAP timeline where three boxes animate in sequence.

  • First, the red box moves 200px to the right.
  • Then, the blue box moves 100px down.
  • Finally, the green box scales up to 1.5 times its original size.

Each animation starts after the previous one ends, showcasing the power of GSAP timelines for sequencing animations.

    🧠 Explanation (Step-by-step)
                    Timeline created
                    Box1 moves right
                    After box1 finishes → box2 moves down
                    After box2 finishes → box3 scales
                    No animation overlaps
