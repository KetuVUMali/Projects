Scroll Down ⬇️
    👉 Explanation: Animate a box when it enters the screen

        🧠 WHAT IS HAPPENING?

            Page is scrollable
            Box is hidden initially
            When you scroll down and box enters viewport → animation starts

    
        Animate a box when its section comes into view

            Page has two full-screen sections
            Box is in the second section, initially hidden
            When you scroll to the second section → box animates into view

    
        🧠 What is happening :
                Page has 2 full screen sections
                When you scroll to section 2
                Box animates from bottom to its place

                📌 scrollTrigger: ".box"
                → means animation starts when .box enters viewport
    
        How it is work :
            1. gsap.from() sets initial state of .box (y:100, opacity:0)
            2. scrollTrigger watches .box
            3. When .box enters viewport during scroll (viewport bottom reaches .box top)
            4. Animation plays to bring .box to y:0, opacity:1
