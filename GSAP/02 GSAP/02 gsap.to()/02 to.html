gsap.to() Method : Animate Movement and Opacity
The gsap.to() method animates an element from its current state to the specified properties.

Syntax: 
            gsap.to(target, {vars});

Example: 
            gsap.to(".box", {x: 150, opacity: 0.3, duration: 1});
This moves the element with class "box" 150 pixels to the right and changes its opacity to 0.3 over 1 second.
