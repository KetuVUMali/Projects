        3️⃣ rotate transforms : Rotate elements.

                rotate() – Rotate elements around a fixed point (default is center).

                rotate(angle) → angle in degrees (deg) or radians (rad).

                Positive values → clockwise rotation
                Negative values → counter-clockwise rotation

                You can combine rotate with other transforms like scale() or translate().

        Example usages:
                rotate(45deg) → Rotate 45 degrees clockwise
                rotate(-30deg) → Rotate 30 degrees counter-clockwise
                rotate(360deg) → Full rotation back to original position
