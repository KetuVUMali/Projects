📌 What is align-items?
    align-items is a FLEXBOX property.
    It controls VERTICAL alignment of items inside a container.

📌 Important:
    align-items works only when:
    - d-flex (flexbox) is applied

1️⃣ align-items-start (Top aligned)

📌 Concept
    Items align from the TOP of container

Image and text are aligned at the TOP. Useful when content should start from the same top line.

2️⃣ align-items-center (Vertically Centered – MOST USED)

📌 Concept
    Items are vertically centered
    Most common real-world use case

This is the BEST and MOST USED alignment. Image stays perfectly centered relative to text. Used in cards, lists, profile sections.

3️⃣ align-items-end (Bottom aligned)

📌 Concept
    Items align at the BOTTOM of container

Image and text align at the BOTTOM. Rarely used, but helpful in specific layouts.

4️⃣ align-items-stretch (Default behavior)

📌 Concept
    Items stretch to fill container height
    Default flex behavior

Content stretches to container height. Mostly noticeable with text blocks.
